## Industrial Training
A deep dive into the footwear manufacturing lifecycle, from material selection to mass production.

### Core Learnings
* **Material Science:** Properties of leather vs. synthetic polymers.
* **Prototyping:** Transitioning from 2D sketches to 3D outsole molds.
* **Quality Assurance:** Stress-testing assembly components for durability.

> *Insight:* The industry is shifting toward sustainable materials and additive manufacturing (3D printing) to reduce waste.
